Patents Statistics

Recently, I came across the patent publications disclosed in World Intellectual Property Organization (WIPO) from 2000 to 2021. This project looks into the number of publications by country, year, applicant and so on.

Patents worldwide

The following bar graphs show the top 20 countries of 2021 and 2000. The countries newly appeared in top 20 are China(Rank 1), India(Rank 14), Turkey(Rank 19) and Singapore(Rank 20).
The countries not appeared in top 20 are Belgium(Rank 16 in 2000), Norway(Rank 17 in 2000), Russia(Rank 18 in 2000) and Spain(Rank 20 in 2000).

The countries with upgraded ranks are South Korea(up 9), Italy(up 1), Israel (up 1), Austria(up 1).
